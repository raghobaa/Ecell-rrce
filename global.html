About Us

Our Vision

To create a world where sustainable water management and environmental stewardship are integral parts of daily life, ensuring a healthier planet for future generations.

Our Mission

To educate, inspire, and empower individuals, communities, and businesses to adopt innovative water conservation techniques and environmentally friendly practices, fostering a culture of sustainability and respect for our natural resources.

Our Goal

To inspire and empower individuals, communities, and organizations to adopt sustainable water management practices and environmentally friendly habits, leading to significant, measurable reductions in water consumption and environmental impact.

Arghyam

Arghyam is a public charitable foundation based in Bangalore, India, dedicated to ensuring safe, sustainable water for all. Established in 2005 by Rohini Nilekani, Arghyam focuses on funding and supporting initiatives related to water and sanitation. The organization's name, which means "offering" in Sanskrit, reflects its mission to contribute to the welfare of society by addressing critical water issues. Click here to contribute
